About User Journey Milestones

Every person’s journey with Spotify is different. While there are certainly commonalities, particularly within specific user and consumer segments, each and every current, lapsed, and future user has had or will have a unique and nuanced relationship with our product and our brand. Each individual’s personal journey is influenced both directly by their experience with Spotify, as well as by outside influences that we do not control.

Rather than striving to map each user journey in all of its nuance, we can focus on key milestones. Once we understand these milestones, we can explore the qualitative and quantitative drivers and barriers to reaching each milestone. By understanding the drivers and barriers to reaching milestones along the journey, we can introduce key features and content to users that will encourage them along their individual journey.
